## Description
VitalDecode helps you compare your blood test results to standard reference ranges — instantly.

**IMPORTANT MEDICAL DISCLAIMER: VitalDecode is a data reference tool, NOT a medical device. It does not provide medical diagnosis, treatment advice, or health assessments. Always seek a doctor's advice in addition to using this app and before making any medical decisions. Do not ignore professional medical advice or delay seeking it because of information provided by this app.**

SCAN AND UNDERSTAND
Point your camera at any blood test report or upload a PDF. Our advanced OCR technology reads and digitizes your results in seconds. No more squinting at tiny numbers or searching for unfamiliar terms.

REFERENCE RANGE COMPARISON
Every biomarker is displayed alongside standard reference ranges AND optimal ranges. See at a glance what is within reference range, what is within optimal range, and what is outside reference range — color-coded for instant clarity.

AI-POWERED DATA COMPARISON
Compare your numbers to standard reference ranges with AI assistance. See which values fall inside or outside published ranges, and get topics to discuss with your healthcare provider at your next visit.

TRACK YOUR DATA
Monitor how your biomarker values change over time with beautiful trend charts. See your data unfold and spot patterns you might otherwise miss.

PRIVACY FIRST
All your data stays on your device. We never upload your health information to our servers. Your results, your control.
