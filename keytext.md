## Description

VitalDecode turns your confusing blood test reports into clear, actionable insights — instantly.

🩸 SCAN & UNDERSTAND
Point your camera at any blood test report or upload a PDF. Our advanced OCR technology reads and digitizes your results in seconds. No more squinting at tiny numbers or Googling unfamiliar terms.

📊 SMART BIOMARKER ANALYSIS
Every biomarker is analyzed against standard reference ranges AND optimal ranges. See at a glance what's normal, what's optimal, and what needs attention — color-coded for instant clarity.

🤖 AI-POWERED INSIGHTS
Get personalized, easy-to-understand explanations of your results powered by AI. Understand what your numbers mean, how they relate to each other, and what you might want to discuss with your doctor.

📈 TRACK YOUR PROGRESS
Monitor how your biomarkers change over time with beautiful trend charts. See your health journey unfold and spot patterns you might otherwise miss.

🔒 PRIVACY FIRST
All your data stays on your device. We never upload your health information to our servers. Your results, your control.

KEY FEATURES:
• Camera scanning with real-time OCR
• PDF document import
• Manual entry for any biomarker
