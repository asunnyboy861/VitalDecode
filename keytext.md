## Description
VitalDecode turns your confusing blood test reports into clear, actionable insights - instantly.

**IMPORTANT: VitalDecode is a data visualization tool, not a medical device. It does not provide medical diagnosis or treatment advice. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for medical interpretation of lab results.**

SCAN AND UNDERSTAND
Point your camera at any blood test report or upload a PDF. Our advanced OCR technology reads and digitizes your results in seconds. No more squinting at tiny numbers or searching for unfamiliar terms.

SMART BIOMARKER ANALYSIS
Every biomarker is displayed alongside standard reference ranges AND optimal ranges. See at a glance what is within standard range, what is within optimal range, and what is outside standard range - color-coded for instant clarity.

AI-POWERED INSIGHTS
Get personalized, easy-to-understand explanations of your results powered by AI. Understand what your numbers mean, how they relate to each other, and what you might want to discuss with your healthcare provider.

TRACK YOUR PROGRESS
Monitor how your biomarkers change over time with beautiful trend charts. See your health data unfold and spot patterns you might otherwise miss.

PRIVACY FIRST
All your data stays on your device. We never upload your health information to our servers. Your results, your control.
