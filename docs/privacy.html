VitalDecode Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 13, 2026

1. Introduction

VitalDecode ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our iOS application.

2. Information We Collect

Data You Provide:

  • Name, age, and gender (optional profile information)
  • Blood test data you scan or manually enter
  • Feedback and support messages

Automatically Collected:

  • Device model and iOS version (for compatibility)
  • App usage analytics (anonymized)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To compare your blood test values to published reference ranges
  • To provide data comparison and trend tracking features
  • To improve our app functionality
  • To respond to support requests

IMPORTANT: VitalDecode does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, treatment advice, or health assessments. Always seek a doctor's advice in addition to using this app and before making any medical decisions.

4. Data Storage and Security

All your health data is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit your blood test results, biomarker data, or personal health information to our servers.

AI Analysis Data Transmission: When you use the AI Data Comparison feature, your health information is sent to OpenAI (api.openai.com) for processing. This includes:

  • Biomarker names, values, units, and reference ranges from your blood test results
  • Your age and gender (from your profile)

This data is processed by OpenAI's gpt-4o-mini model to generate data comparisons against reference ranges. OpenAI uses this data only to provide the analysis service and does not use it for training their AI models. You must explicitly consent to this data transmission before each analysis session. You can revoke this consent at any time in Settings. The AI comparison does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment recommendations.

For more information, see OpenAI's Privacy Policy.

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data on-device.

5. HealthKit Data

If you choose to enable HealthKit integration, VitalDecode may read and write lab result data to Apple's Health app. This data remains under your control through Apple's Health privacy settings. We never share HealthKit data with third parties.

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Apple HealthKit: For health data integration (optional). We never share HealthKit data with third parties.
  • OpenAI: For AI-powered analysis. Your health information (biomarker values, reference ranges, age, gender) is transmitted to OpenAI (api.openai.com) for processing. OpenAI provides equivalent data protection and does not use your data for AI training. OpenAI Privacy Policy
  • Apple StoreKit: For subscription management. No personal data is shared.

7. Data Retention

All data is stored on your device until you delete it. You can delete all data at any time through the app's Settings. Uninstalling the app removes all stored data.

8. Children's Privacy

VitalDecode is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data stored in the app
  • Export your data in CSV format
  • Delete your data at any time
  • Opt out of HealthKit integration
  • Revoke AI data consent at any time in Settings → AI Analysis

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes through the app.
