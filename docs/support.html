Frequently Asked Questions

How do I scan a blood test report?

Tap the "Scan with Camera" button on the Scan tab to take a photo of your report, or use "Upload PDF" to import a digital copy. You can also enter values manually.

Is my health data safe?

Yes! All your data is stored locally on your device. We never transmit your health data to our servers. If you use AI analysis, data is sent directly to OpenAI using your own API key.

How many free scans do I get?

Free users receive 3 complimentary scans. After that, you can upgrade to Pro for unlimited scans, AI analysis, and advanced features.

How do I set up AI analysis?

Go to Settings > AI Analysis and enter your OpenAI API key. Pro subscribers can use the built-in AI features without their own key.

Can I export my data?

Yes! Go to Settings > Data > Export All Reports to export your biomarker data as a CSV file.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions on your iPhone, or open the App Store and tap your profile to manage subscriptions.

Does VitalDecode provide medical advice?

No. VitalDecode is for informational purposes only. Always consult a qualified healthcare provider for medical decisions.
